THE COMPANY

BOUILLANT

Bouillant! is a young startup created around the love of home cooking. Founded by Julie and Thomas, Bouillant! aim to share a passion about homemade food and grandmother recipe. Bouillant! proposes sharing cooking lesson experiences from enthusiastic cooks to curious customers.

PROJECT

OVERVIEW

I've worked as freelancer in a 2 weeks sprint. I was asked to create a platform for Bouillant! customers so that they could create and manage their own experiences easily. In two weeks, I've run interviews and usability tests and deliver a high-fidelity MVP ready to develop. I've followed Design Thinking approach in order to empathize and proposed a solution wanted by the user. In two weeks, I create a flow where cooks can create and modify their experiences, a calendar so that they can keep track of their schedule, and a dashboard to manage them.
